Hello, people from the future! I am Sujan, a PhD student at RIT in Computing and Information Sciences. My PhD advisor is Dr. Ashique KhudaBukhsh. My research is dedicated to extracting social insights through NLP tools and developing reliable AI systems. My work has been recognized at top AI conferences, including IJCAI and EMNLP. Beyond academia, my role as an educational content creator at Normalized Nerd allows me to share my knowledge in machine learning and data science, impacting a global audience of over 97,000 subscribers.
